Create a professional “Open Account (Kanè)” onboarding system for Pyram Sterling Bank.

When a visitor clicks “Open an Account (Ouvè yon Kont)”, display a modern and professional account opening page.

The registration form must collect the following information:

* First Name
* Last Name
* Phone Number
* Haitian National ID Number

Display the account opening fee as:

Account Opening Fee: 500 HTG

Explain that:

* 250 HTG is used to create the customer’s Bank Account (Kanè) and Pyram Sterling Bank Card.
* 250 HTG is deposited into the customer’s new account as the initial balance after the account is approved and activated.

Add a mandatory checkbox with the following text:

“I agree to pay the 500 HTG account opening fee.”

The applicant cannot continue unless the checkbox is selected.

After completing the form, the applicant must schedule an appointment by selecting:

* Appointment Date
* Appointment Time

When the form is submitted, the application must automatically appear in the Admin Dashboard with the status Pending Approval.

The administrator must be able to:

* View every application
* Review the applicant’s information
* Approve the application
* Reject the application

The account must NOT become active until:

* The applicant appears at the bank.
* The applicant’s identity is verified.
* The applicant pays the 500 HTG account opening fee.

After approval, the system must automatically generate:

* A unique Bank Account Number (Kanè Number)
* A unique Pyram Sterling Bank Virtual Card Number
* A starting Credit Score
* A Wallet linked to the account
* An initial balance of 250 HTG

Immediately after activation, the customer must be able to log into the application and see:

* Their full name
* Their Account Number
* Their Virtual Card
* Their Credit Score
* Their available balance of 250 HTG
* Their transaction history (empty if no transaction has been made yet)

After the account is activated, automatically display a professional welcome message:

“Congratulations! Your Pyram Sterling Bank account has been successfully activated. Welcome to Pyram Sterling Bank.”

The system must also send this same welcome message as an in-app notification.

The onboarding experience must look and behave like a real professional digital bank, with a secure, clean, modern, and user-friendly interface.
